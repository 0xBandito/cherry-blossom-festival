001 / Heritage

The Cherry Blossom Capital of the World

Since 1982, Macon has celebrated the breathtaking bloom of over 350,000 Yoshino cherry trees—more than any other city on earth. What began as a community celebration has grown into ten days of art, music, food, and natural wonder that draws visitors from across the globe.

002 / Experience

Ten Days of Wonder

From the Pink Parade through historic downtown to live concerts under canopies of blossoms, the festival transforms Macon into a celebration of spring itself. Enjoy curated art exhibitions, chef-driven food experiences, family activities, and twilight garden tours.
